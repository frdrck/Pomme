      How to Play

      Pomme is an image pairing game where the goal is to make the best combination of images.

      Each round, a new "match" image appears, and a different player gets to be judge. Players select the best image from their hand to accompany the match. Then the judge decides which of these images is the best. If the judge is idle, players vote. The winner gets a point. Most games go to 10 points.
